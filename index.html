Are AI Chatbots Proficient at Tracing CS1 Code?

This was a research paper written alongside two researchers at the University of Adelaide. We researched the suitability of Artificial Intelligence and Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT's ability to complete code tracing exercises. Having an understanding of LLMs' ability to do this lets us consider their use in Computer Science curricula and the effect LLMs have on students. Students are most likely already using these tools to enhance their learning, and incorporating these tools could continue to be beneficial for their learning.
This paper is currently under review, hence no author names have been mentioned
